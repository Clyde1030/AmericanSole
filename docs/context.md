American Boot is a shoes manufacturer In San Antonio Texas, who collaborate with its overseas vendor Tunlite. Tunlite manufactures shoe upper and lasts and send over to American Sole to finish the last step of the procedures, sole attaching. Since Tunlite is in china, every semi product is shipped by ocean or air freight. So there is a need to keep track of the freight. American Customers,usually shoes brands, will place purchase order to American Sole as well. So we will keep track of the order information.
